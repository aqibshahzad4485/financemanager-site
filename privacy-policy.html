Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 20, 2026

At Finance Manager, we believe your financial data is strictly your business. This Privacy Policy outlines our uncompromising approach to data security, collection, and your rights when using our application and website.

1. Zero Telemetry & Local Storage

Finance Manager is architected as an offline-first mobile application. This means every piece of financial data—including your accounts, multi-currency transactions, loan ledgers, and budget limits—is stored securely and exclusively on your local device's storage infrastructure.

We do not operate backend databases. We do not sync your data to our servers. We do not run background telemetry or analytics trackers.

2. Information Collection

Because the app operates independently of any central server, we collect absolutely zero personal financial information from the application. The only data we might collect is standard web logging when you visit this promotional website (financemanager.aqibs.dev), which may include your IP address, browser type, and navigation paths. This data is solely used to maintain website stability and analyze aggregate visitor trends.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We cannot sell, rent, or share your financial data because we simply do not possess it. If you utilize third-party cloud backup solutions provided by your operating system (e.g., Google Drive Android Backups), your data may be subject to their respective privacy policies.

4. Security Protocols

While we ensure the application code is robust and secure (and openly verifiable on GitHub), the ultimate security of your financial data relies on the physical and digital security of your personal device. We recommend utilizing strong device passwords and native encryption methods to safeguard your ledger.

5. External Links

Our website and repository may link out to external resources or third-party platforms (like GitHub). Navigating to these external sites means you are no longer governed by our Privacy Policy. We advise reviewing the privacy terms of any third-party service you interact with.

6. Policy Updates

As the application evolves, so might this policy. Any modifications to our privacy practices will be documented on this page. For significant changes, we may notify users via application release notes.
